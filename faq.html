Frequently Asked Questions

What is FALCON's Cipher SphinX?
FALCON's Cipher SphinX is an encryption and decryption platform designed to provide users with advanced tools to protect their data.
Which encryption algorithms are supported?
Our platform supports AES, DES, 3DES, Blowfish, ChaCha20, CAST5, RC2, Caesar Cipher, Atbash Cipher, and XOR Cipher.
Is FALCON's Cipher SphinX free to use?
Yes, FALCON's Cipher SphinX is completely free to use. Our goal is to provide privacy and security tools to everyone.
How can I encrypt my data?
Simply enter your data into our encryption tool, choose the desired algorithm, and the platform will handle the rest. It's quick and secure!
Can I decrypt previously encrypted data?
Yes! You can decrypt any data that was encrypted using our platform as long as you have the correct key.
